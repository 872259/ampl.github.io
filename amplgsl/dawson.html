Dawson Function¶

The Dawson integral is defined by \(\exp(-x^2) \int_0^x \exp(t^2) dt\). A table of Dawson’s integral can be found in Abramowitz & Stegun, Table 7.5.

gsl_sf_dawson(x)¶

This routine computes the value of Dawson’s integral for \(x\).
